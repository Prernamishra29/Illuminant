About Illuminant Vitam

Where Insight Meets Impact

At Illuminant Vitam Solutions, LLC, we believe that meaningful business transformation begins with clarity. Our name — Illuminant (to light the way) and Vitam (Latin for life) — reflects our mission: to bring illumination and vitality to every facet of your brand and business.

Based in Sarasota, Florida, we are a strategic marketing consultancy dedicated to helping businesses elevate their operations, refine their messaging, and create sustainable growth strategies that align with both vision and values.

Whether you're an emerging entrepreneur or an evolving company, we help you bridge the gap between your potential and your performance.

What We Do

We help purpose-driven businesses:

  • Uncover operational blind spots and turn chaos into clarity
  • Design strategic brand experiences that inspire trust and engagement
  • Align internal systems with external growth for sustainable momentum
  • Create intentional messaging that resonates deeply with ideal clients

Why We Exist

Too often, founders and businesses get stuck in the noise — scattered strategies, inconsistent branding, and lack of alignment between their vision and their execution. At Illuminant Vitam Solutions, LLC, we don't just provide services — we provide insight. Insight that leads to impact.

We're here to help you connect the dots, simplify your processes, and rise above the ordinary with a distinct creative edge.

Meet the Founder

Waxela Sananda is a creative entrepreneur and strategic visionary with over 30 years of experience building businesses across both the online and brick-and-mortar space. Her journey includes launching and scaling multiple brands, including a 15-year run as a successful retail store owner.

Waxela brings a unique blend of intuitive clarity and business acumen to her work — combining marketing strategy, creative storytelling, and operational refinement to help brands grow with authenticity and precision.

Our Philosophy

  • Strategic Alignment – Everything starts with your why
  • Creative Precision – Bold ideas deserve thoughtful execution
  • Sustainable Growth – We don't chase trends — we build legacies

Service 1: Startup & Enterprise Growth Support

Clarity, structure, and momentum for every stage of your growth.

Through Creative Brand Solutions, we help you align your visual identity, messaging, and storytelling with your strategic goals. We don't just make things look good — we make them work across every touchpoint, from your website to your social media, email, packaging, and beyond. Whether you're building from the ground up or refreshing a legacy brand, we create a cohesive ecosystem that reflects your purpose, resonates with your audience, and builds trust through clarity and consistency. Expect a blend of:

  • Strategic brand audits
  • Messaging refinement
  • Messaging refinement
  • Visual direction + brand identity alignment
  • Storytelling that connects emotionally and positions you powerfully

The result? A brand that looks, sounds, and feels like the true you — elevated, unified, and ready to grow.

Service 3: Strategic Marketing Consulting

Purposeful strategies that elevate your brand and move your audience to act.

In a crowded market, it's not enough to just "get seen." You need a strategy that speaks directly to your ideal audience — and keeps them coming back. Our Strategic Marketing Consulting services are designed to do exactly that. We work closely with you to build and implement marketing strategies that align with your unique business goals, elevate brand visibility, and deepen customer engagement. Whether you're launching a new offer, repositioning your brand, or refining your existing campaigns, we help you craft marketing that is intentional, consistent, and results-driven. We focus on:

  • Tailored campaign strategy + planning
  • Messaging frameworks that connect and convert
  • Multi-platform integration across digital and offline channels
  • Brand positioning that builds long-term loyalty

You'll leave with a clear path forward, custom strategies that reflect your brand's essence, and the tools to execute with confidence. This isn't one-size-fits-all. It's strategic clarity that honors your brand's vision and helps you grow with purpose.

Service 4: Operational Optimization

Clear systems. Smarter workflows. Sustainable growth.

Behind every thriving business is a foundation of systems that actually work — not just in theory, but in real time. Our Operational Optimization service is designed to help you uncover what's not working, why it's slowing you down, and how to fix it in a way that aligns with your goals. Through process audits, data-driven insights, and performance metrics, we illuminate the operational blind spots holding your business back and provide strategic solutions that make your backend as strong as your front-facing brand. This is not just about efficiency — it's about scalability. It's about building systems that give you time back, reduce friction, and make it easier to serve your clients well. We focus on:

  • Operational audits to assess performance and workflow gaps
  • Systems strategy for more efficient delivery and execution
  • Performance tracking tied to your actual business goals
  • Custom solutions that grow with your team and your vision

If you've grown faster than your systems can handle, or if you're ready to scale but your foundation feels shaky — this offering helps you regain control, improve flow, and create lasting infrastructure that supports your next level.

Service 5: Digital Transformation Strategy

Modernize your systems. Elevate your customer experience. Future-proof your business.

Technology is no longer optional — it's integral to sustainable growth. But adopting digital tools without strategy leads to complexity, not clarity. Our Digital Transformation Strategy offering helps your business embrace the right technologies to streamline operations, improve customer engagement, and ignite innovation — all aligned with your brand's unique goals and growth stage. We don't just plug in tools and hope for the best. We take a holistic, consultative approach, ensuring that your digital systems are designed to support your vision, enhance your user experience, and scale with you. We guide you through:

  • Evaluating and selecting digital tools that align with your objectives
  • Creating smart, integrated systems for marketing, operations, and delivery
  • Improving digital customer journeys across platforms.
  • Laying the groundwork for automation, optimization, and innovation

Whether you're digitizing legacy systems or starting fresh with scalable tech, we ensure your brand is positioned to grow, compete, and thrive in today's fast-moving digital landscape.

Ongoing support, strategic insight, and a partner who evolves with your brand.

Some growth journeys can't be rushed — and shouldn't be done alone.

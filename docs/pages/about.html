About Illuminant Vitam Solutions

Where Insight Meets Impact

At Illuminant Vitam Solutions, LLC, we believe that meaningful business transformation begins with clarity. Our name — Illuminant (to light the way) and Vitam (Latin for life) — reflects our mission: to bring illumination and vitality to every facet of your brand and business.

Based in Sarasota, Florida, we are a strategic marketing consultancy dedicated to helping businesses elevate their operations, refine their messaging, and create sustainable growth strategies that align with both vision and values.

Whether you're an emerging entrepreneur or an evolving company, we help you bridge the gap between your potential and your performance.

Vision-Led Strategy

We align your operations with purpose, helping you see and reach your potential.

Creative Precision

Our brand storytelling and design tactics make your message unforgettable.

Sustainable Growth

We help build systems that scale gracefully and authentically over time.

  • Uncover operational blind spots and turn chaos into clarity

Meet the Founder

Waxela Sananda is a creative entrepreneur and strategic visionary with over 30 years of experience building businesses across both the online and brick-and-mortar space.

Her journey includes launching and scaling multiple brands, including a 15-year run as a successful retail store owner.

Waxela brings a unique blend of intuitive clarity and business acumen to her work — combining marketing strategy, creative storytelling, and operational refinement to help brands grow with authenticity and precision.

Her mission through Illuminant Vitam Solutions, LLC is to serve purpose-driven leaders who are ready to modernize, align, and expand their impact.
